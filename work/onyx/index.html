Onyx

Onyx CenterSource is a leading global provider of B2B payments and business intelligence solutions to the hospitality industry.

Event Management

Hotels use commissions to build long-term relationships with event managers and event planners and encourage them to use their facilities and services in the future. Onyx handled the commissions process and the payment back and forth between hotel chains and event managers. Onyx wanted to know if there were any revenue opportunities by expanding into the event marketplace. The Onyx executive team tapped Slalom to present a proposal regarding this opportunity.

Six Weeks

That was all the time we were given to do the discovery and design and present a business requirements document to the Onyx executive team. I had to carefully schedule every step of my user experience process right from the start. The key was to start with the outcome in mind. I then walked back week by week to figure out what Product artifacts we would need each week to get to the final deliverable.

Prototype

To show Onyx what a possible solution could look like, I created a prototype in code using the BFF prototyping engine, a prototyping tool that I had developed to help my Product team ideate much faster than was traditionally possible (see Securus for additional information).

Conclusion

This project was a pilot for Onyx to see if it was worth it to pursue it further. Onyx was in the commissions business; event management was completely new to them. My prototype and the BRD we included as part of our presentation proved to Onyx's exec team that there was an opportunity to expand beyond commissions and into the events industry. As a result of this project, Onyx awarded Slalom additional funding for a small development team to convert my prototype into a full-blown product.
