About Me

I am a Product Manager with deep hands on experience in both product design and product development. As a product manager my main tools are Jira, Confluence, Miro, FigJam, and DoveTail; and qualitative & quantitative analytics tools like FullStory, Google Analytics, and Azure Telemetry.

When I'm diving into design I primarily use Figma, as I'm a seasoned user, having used it for over 5 years. In the past I've used tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, Axure, Balsamiq, and others.

I like to prototype my own ideas in code using primarily JavaScript, including libraries and frameworks such as React and Svelte. I have experimented with other languages in the past.

I have built teams from the ground up and led teams; designed and built websites, web apps, native apps, mobile apps, design systems, and even the occasional API. I've worked with scrum teams around the world including USA, Canada, Mexico, Ukraine, Germany, and India.

I've advised several startups on their product design and journey including an agentic AI company called Yobi based out of Silicon Valley.
